About

Julia Haeun Kim graduated with an honors bachelors in Public Health from Oregon State University with a focus in Health Promotion and Health Behavior. She has two minors in Computer Science and Education. She is a classically-trained cellist who has performed in multiple ensembles, such as the Corvallis-OSU Symphony Orchestra, the Portland Youth Philharmonic, and the OSU Hulsman String Ensemble.
